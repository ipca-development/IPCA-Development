0.2 Revision System

The Organization Management Manual and its amendments are proposed, drafted, issued, accepted and implemented by the Head of Training after a BCAA spot check.

The Compliance Monitoring Manager ensures that the content of the Organization Management Manual complies with all applicable regulations and requirements.

Any amendment proposal by the Head of Training shall be executed by sending an e-mail to the Compliance Monitoring Manager and the Accountable Manager. The e-mail shall include the revised draft of the applicable manual, section or paragraph, as well as the reason(s) for the amendment.

Upon verification of the draft by the Compliance Monitoring Manager, the amendment shall be sent to the BCAA.

All amendments shall be accompanied by an updated List of Effective Parts. Changes, additions or deletions are identified by a vertical bar placed in front of the revised text.

A “Highlight of Changes” paragraph shall contain a full history of changes between revisions.

Digital, password-protected PDF versions of the Organization Management Manual may be distributed and shall be marked “Uncontrolled Copy” on each page and protected against printing.

A Temporary Revision Procedure is not applicable within EuroPilot Center B/ATO-017.
